Suggested sites

Click on either the image or the text below to be taken to the suggested site.

TED.com

A great way to learn English is to simply listen to it! You can use this site to listen to short, informative stories. The advantage is that you can hear what English sounds like in a descriptive or humorous tone. Videos tend to be short and fun.

CBC News - Video

True Canadian English! CBC is a Canadian news network. They have thousands of video stories. Best part is - they speak in Canadian English dialect and have CC (closed captioning/subtitles), so you can easily follow.

Free ESL classes! Latest ISMC brochure

International Students Ministries Canada brochure lists free ESL locations and times. It doesn't matter what faith you have - the ESL classes are secular (non-religious) and free.
